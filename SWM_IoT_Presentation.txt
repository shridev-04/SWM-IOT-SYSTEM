## Slide 4: The Dynamic Temperature Algorithm (The Formula)
**Slide Text:**
* **Formula Logic:** `Ideal Target Temp = f(Weather Temp, Humidity, User Limits)`
* The ESP32 calculates the target water temperature using an inverse relationship logic to the outside weather.

**Formula Rules:**
1. **If Hot Outside (>= 25°C):** Calculate cooling intensity. Target ranges between 5°C to 12°C.
2. **If Cold Outside (< 25°C):** Calculate heating intensity. Target ranges between 28°C to 45°C.
3. **Humidity Factor:** Adjusts Target by 2-3°C based on perceived heat (Feels Like).

**Speaker Notes (Judges ko kya bolna hai):**
"Ab aate hain iske sabse main dimag (logic) par. System pehle OpenWeather API se bahar ka 'Feels Like' temperature aur humidity nikalta hai. Agar bahar garmi (25°C se zyada) hai, toh yeh formula target paani ko thanda (5-12°C) karne ki range nikalta hai. Agar sardi hai, toh yeh target ko 28-45°C ki range me set karta hai."

---

## Slide 5: Step-by-Step Formula Example
**Slide Text:**
* **Scenario:** Outside Temperature = 35°C (Hot), Humidity = 60%.
* **Step 1 (Check Weather):** 35°C is >= 25°C. System enters COOLING Mode.
* **Step 2 (Heat Intensity):** `(35 - 25) / 25 = 0.40 (40% Heat Intensity)`
* **Step 3 (Base Target):** `12°C - (0.40 * (12 - 5))` = `12 - 2.8` = `9.2°C`
* **Step 4 (Humidity Adjustment):** `- (60 / 100 * 3)` = `- 1.8°C`
* **Final Target Temp:** `9.2 - 1.8 = 7.4°C`

**Speaker Notes (Judges ko kya bolna hai):**
"Ise ek example se samajhte hain. Maan lijiye bahar 35 degree temperature aur 60% humidity hai. 
Step 1: Formula dekhega ki garmi hai, toh cooling mode activate hoga.
Step 2: Kitni garmi hai? 40% intensity aayi.
Step 3: Woh cooling range (12 se 5) me is intensity ko apply karega, jisse answer 9.2°C aayega.
Step 4: Kyuki humidity 60% hai toh paani ko thoda aur thanda karna padega, isliye usne 1.8 degree aur minus kiya.
Final Target aaya 7.4°C. Yeh lamba calculation ESP32 khud automatically process karta hai!"

## Slide 6: The 2°C Hysteresis Deadband (Energy Saving Concept)

*(Insert Hysteresis Graph Image Here)*

**Slide Text:**
* **The Problem:** Relays turning ON/OFF continuously around the exact target temperature wastes electricity and destroys the compressor.
* **The Fix:** ±2°C Hysteresis Deadband.
* **Example:** If Target is 10°C, cooling starts only at **12°C** and stops precisely at **10°C**. It will not restart until water heats back up to 12°C.

**Speaker Notes (Judges ko kya bolna hai):**
"Energy save karne aur hardware ki life badhane ke liye humne 2 degree Hysteresis concept lagaya hai. Agar target 10°C hai, toh cooling thik 10 par band ho jayegi, par dobara tab tak start nahi hogi jab tak temp wapas 12°C na ho jaye. Is beech compressor ko aaram milta hai aur kafi power save hoti hai."

## Slide 7: Safety & Emergency Protocols
**Slide Text:**
* **App-Triggered Emergency:** Single button to instantly cut power to all heating and cooling relays.
* **Visual Alert:** App turns solid Red; ESP32 LCD bypasses all data to display a constant "SYSTEM SHUTDOWN" alert.
* **Fail-Safe Mechanism:** Minimum cycle delays (1 minute) hardcoded in ESP32 to prevent compressor short-cycling even under rapid user inputs.

**Speaker Notes (Judges ko kya bolna hai):**
"Safety humari top priority hai. Agar koi fault aata hai toh User app se 1-click me 'Emergency Shutdown' kar sakta hai. Command milte hi ESP32 turant relays band kar deta hai aur uski LCD par 'EMERGENCY' ka constant alert aane lagta hai. Iske alawa, compressor ko kharab hone se bachane ke liye firmware me 1-minute ka cycle-delay lock bhi lagaya gaya hai."
